## What is MDMA and Why Do People Seek It?
MDMA, commonly known as ecstasy or molly, is a synthetic psychoactive drug that acts as both a stimulant and hallucinogen. First synthesized in 1912, it gained popularity in the 1980s and 1990s within rave and club scenes for its euphoric effects, increased empathy, and heightened sensory experiences.

People often search for MDMA buying options due to its perceived benefits in social settings or therapeutic contexts. However, in 2026, research continues to explore its potential in controlled medical environments, such as PTSD treatment under FDA trials, but recreational use remains strictly prohibited.

### Historical Context and Cultural Impact
MDMA's rise coincided with electronic music festivals, but crackdowns in the 1980s led to its classification as illegal. Today, global enforcement targets dark web markets and street distribution, emphasizing public health over prohibition alone.

### What are the signs of contaminated MDMA?

### How can I get help if I'm considering buying MDMA?
